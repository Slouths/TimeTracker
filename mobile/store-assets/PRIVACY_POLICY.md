# Privacy Policy for TradeTimer

**Last Updated:** November 3, 2025

## Introduction

Welcome to TradeTimer. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application and services.

By using TradeTimer, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We collect the following personal information when you create an account and use our services:

**Account Information:**

- Profile picture (optional)
- Business/company name (optional)

**Client Information:**

**Time Tracking Data:**

**Invoice Data:**
- Invoice numbers
- Invoice amounts
- Payment terms
- Invoice dates
- Business details (your information)

### Automatically Collected Information

**Device Information:**

- Operating system version (iOS/Android)

**Usage Information:**
- Features used within the app
- Frequency of use
- Session duration

**Technical Information:**
- IP address

### Biometric Information

If you enable biometric authentication (Face ID, Touch ID, or fingerprint):

- Biometric authentication is handled entirely by your device's secure enclave
- We only receive a success/failure response from your device

## How We Store Your Information

### Data Storage

**Cloud Infrastructure:**
- Your data is stored on Supabase (https://supabase.com)
- Hosted on secure AWS servers
- Located in United States data centers
- Regular backups performed automatically

**Encryption:**
- Data encrypted in transit using TLS/HTTPS
- Data encrypted at rest using industry-standard encryption
- Passwords hashed using bcrypt
- Secure token-based authentication

**Local Storage:**
- Some data cached locally on your device for offline access
- Local data encrypted using device security features
- Automatically synced when internet connection available

### Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services:

- **Active accounts:** Data retained indefinitely while account is active
- **Inactive accounts:** Data may be deleted after 2 years of inactivity
- **Deleted accounts:** Data deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Legal requirements:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law

## How We Share Your Information

We do NOT sell your personal information to third parties.

### Third-Party Service Providers

We share your information with trusted service providers who assist us in operating our services:

**Supabase (Database & Authentication)**
- Purpose: Store and manage your data
- Data shared: All user data
- Privacy policy: https://supabase.com/privacy

**Stripe (Payment Processing)**
- Purpose: Process Pro subscription payments
- Data shared: Email, payment information
- Privacy policy: https://stripe.com/privacy
- Note: Credit card information never stored on our servers

**PostHog (Analytics)** - Optional
- Purpose: Understand app usage and improve features
- Data shared: Anonymous usage data, device information
- Privacy policy: https://posthog.com/privacy
- Note: Can be opted out in Settings

**Sentry (Error Tracking)** - Optional
- Purpose: Monitor and fix crashes and errors
- Data shared: Error logs, device information, app state
- Privacy policy: https://sentry.io/privacy/
- Note: No personal identifiable information included

## GDPR Rights (European Union Users)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the following rights:

**Right to Access:**
- Request confirmation of data processing
- Obtain a copy of your personal data

**Right to Rectification:**
- Correct inaccurate personal data
- Complete incomplete personal data

**Right to Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"):**
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Applicable in certain circumstances

**Right to Restriction:**
- Restrict processing of your personal data
- In specific situations

**Right to Data Portability:**
- Receive your data in a portable format
- Transmit data to another controller

**Right to Object:**
- Object to processing of your personal data
- For direct marketing or other purposes

**Right to Withdraw Consent:**
- Withdraw consent at any time
- Does not affect lawfulness of prior processing

**Right to Lodge a Complaint:**
- File a complaint with your local data protection authority

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@tradetimer.com
